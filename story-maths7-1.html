Captain Nemo commanded a submarine that could dive deep into the ocean and rise to the surface. The surface of the ocean was marked as 0. On a research mission, he first dove to -45 meters to study a coral reef, then descended another 30 meters to explore a shipwreck.

💡 Concept Check

What is the submarine's new position, and how is this calculated using integer addition?

❌ Not quite! NCERT Explanation:

-75 meters. Calculated as (-45) + (-30) = -75. Descending further means adding a negative integer.

✅ Excellent! +100 Points!

From the shipwreck at -75 meters, Captain Nemo needed to rise to -20 meters to collect water samples.

💡 Concept Check

What is the change in depth, and how is this represented as integer subtraction?

❌ Not quite! NCERT Explanation:

Change = +55 meters. Represented as (-20) - (-75) = -20 + 75 = 55. Subtracting a negative is adding its positive.

✅ Excellent! +100 Points!

The submarine had to avoid an underwater mountain at -50 meters. The sonar showed the submarine at -30 meters.

💡 Concept Check

What is the absolute difference between the submarine's depth and the mountain's depth, and why is absolute value important here?

❌ Not quite! NCERT Explanation:

Absolute difference = |-30 - (-50)| = |20| = 20 meters. Absolute value is important because physical distance is always positive.

✅ Excellent! +100 Points!

During a storm, the submarine's temperature control system malfunctioned. The internal temperature was 5°C, but it dropped by 12°C.

💡 Concept Check

What is the final temperature, and how does this show subtraction of integers?

❌ Not quite! NCERT Explanation:

Final temperature = 5 - 12 = -7°C. Subtracting a larger positive from a smaller positive gives a negative integer.

✅ Excellent! +100 Points!

Over a week, the submarine recorded daily depth changes: +15 m, -8 m, +22 m, -30 m, +10 m, -5 m, +6 m. Starting from -100 m, they calculated the final depth.

💡 Concept Check

What is the final depth after these changes?

❌ Not quite! NCERT Explanation:

Sum of changes = 15 - 8 + 22 - 30 + 10 - 5 + 6 = 10. Final depth = -100 + 10 = -90 meters.
